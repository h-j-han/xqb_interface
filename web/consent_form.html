Project Title: Word-by-Word Question Answering with Simultaneous Machine Translation

About the Study

This research is being conducted by Jordan Boyd-Graber at the University of Maryland, College Park. We are inviting you to participate in this research project because you can help answering questions that are translated by either human or machine translator. The purpose of this research project is to investigate how the quality of machine translation impacts the human performance on question answering task.

Procedures

The procedures involve first signing up on the website with an email and password. You will be instructed to play word-by-word question answering game, Quizbowl.

You will be instructed to answer the incremental word-by-word questions, where the question text in source langauge and its translation will be presented incrementally one word at a time to the participants. The questions are from Poland division of International Academic Competitions and translated from Polish to English with simultaneous machine translation, which is restricted use.

Potential Risks and Discomforts

Potential Benefits

There are no known direct benefits from participating in this research. We hope that, in the future, other people might benefit from this study through improved understanding of question answering.
